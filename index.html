What We Offer

This are the main services offered by IZZI Clearing and Forwarding CO.LTD Tanzania.

Inland Delivery

We provide delivery service to your nearest city centre. Delivery to Tanzania mainland regions will be via driver and delivery to Zanzibar will be done via ferry. Customers will only need to pay the fee and wait for their vehicle at their desired city or town centres.

Customs Clearance

Our professional clearing officers will help you clear your car at the port and do all necessary documentation on your behalf. Car clearance at the port requires a lot of time and financial resources but our experienced staff will do all these at an affordable rate. Please contact us to know the rate at which we can clear your vehicle.

Tax Consultation

In Tanzania, the amount of the import duty depends on the types of vehicle as well as the fuel consumption and the year of manufacture. If you need to know drive total cost of your car including import duty and other expenses, please have a contact with us by email, phone or at office.

Car Registration

After your car is cleared and inspected, the next step is to get it registered at the vehicle registry office. Our professional team will help you lodge in documents to the TRA registration system, apply for card and plate numbers and deliver the car to you fully registered.

About Us

IZZI C & F is Tanzanian based service provider which acts as middlemen into delivering desired automobiles to its customers. IZZI C&F works in partnership with CAYMAN PREMIUM LTD, a subsidiary company of JapanCars Co., Ltd in Tanzania.

IZZI C&F was Registered in 2021 under Business Registration and Licensing Agency (BRELA) and therefore compiles to all the legal obligations as per the country as well as business.

Our staff is a team of creative, professional and highly skilled people who work hard on a daily basis to ensure we satisfy the needs of our customers as well as seeking solutions for every query that comes into our way.

Frequently Ask Questions

How much does it cost for clearing of my vehicle?

The clearing agency fee is Tsh 275,000 including VAT for any kind of vehicle.

What am i suppossed to bring into IZZI clearing and forwading agent?

You need to give the agent your TIN number and ID copy.

Can you deliver vehicle to my nearest town or city?

Yes. We have delivery service to all regions across mainland Tanzania and Zanzibar. Please contact us to know exact delivery fee to your nearest city or town.

What am I supposed to do after the vessel arrival?

Our agent will help you calculate important duty and other taxes. After you made the payment of those charges, your vehicle will be released from port ready for registration.

Is clearing and forwarding service's price negotiable?

Yes it is. Please contact us or visitour office regarding service charge discount.

What other charges am i supposed to pay?

In addition to import duty and our clearing fee, you need to pay port charges, shipping line charges, insuarance, plate number fee and vehicle registration fee before you start driving. The port charge depends on the size of your vehicle and CIF value. The shipping line charge depends on shipping company. The plate number charge is Tsh 40,000. The registration fee depends on engine consumption of your vehicle.
